Recollection

A game about finding the present self in the past

Problem

Independently developed videogames are a rarity in South Africa, due to a very small market for locally-produced digital content. Found even less is videogames that are based in the realm of the African story, which has so many unique narratives to tell to South Africans and the world.

Brief

Create a platformer game with a narrative centered around an African folktale, which forms the basis of the game’s design and narrative design. The game should feature a series of obstacles and enemies which can present a challenge to the player.

Solution

Recollection is a story-driven platformer game designed to combine a strong narrative and entertaining gameplay for a unique experience. The game follows the story of Bai and how he must overcome countless obstacles in order to save his father.

Along the way he will have face corrupted beasts, deadly traps and desirous siblings vying for their share of their father’s glory.

The Folktale

Bai is told that his Father died in the hunt, but he refuses to believe it. He is a very small boy, but he has a huge heart and thus sets out on a quest to find his lost Father. He has to find every piece of his Father; his bones, flesh, blood, breath and voice in order to bring him back to life.

On his journey, the skeleton of his Father will help him to fight off enemies that stand in his way, fuelled by his memories. Each part of his Father is kept in a terrible realm that encapsulates the essence of the part required.

The realm is guarded over by Bai's jealous older Brothers who want to keep the glory of saving their Father all to themselves. Each Brother is so infatuated with their contribution, that they shaped their domains to be a monument to them.

Bai

Bai, like so many boys, has always looked up to his father. He refuses to believe that his father is dead, and will do everything in his power to prove his brothers wrong.

Bai is determined to find each and every part of his father, in order to bring him back, no matter the cost.

The Father

Bai’s father is a hunter and head of their family. In a fateful hunt, he perishes, leaving behind Bai and his five older brothers.

He vows to protect his son at all times, to repay him for his unwavering loyalty. The father can exist because his memory is kept alive in the mind of Bai.

The Brother

He is the youngest of Bai’s elder brothers, who is determined to stop Bai in his quest to bring back their father, in order for him to win his father’s adoration.

He stands in the way of Bai being able to pass to the next section of his quest, and must therefore be defeated. He lives in the realm of the bones, a dark and terrifying monument to all things dead and decaying.

Bone Beasts

These terrible creatures tower above the diminutive Bai, serving the will of Bai’s brother. They consist of a mismatch of bones and parts from a number of dead beasts.

They have only one directive – kill Bai.

Traps and Obstacles




Spike Pits

Obstacles such as spike pits will make it devastating for the character to fall off specific platforms as these broken bones will slowly kill Bai. These spikes will also be placed on walls to stop Bai from over jumping specific obstacles.



Drop Spikes

These spike platforms will come down from the ceiling; if you happen to get caught under one of these it will slowly start to kill Bai. You will have to run a gauntlet to get past these three obstacles.

Breaking Platforms

For these platform you will be able to notice a slight crack in the centre of them. However if you are moving to fast they will go unnoticed and the player might fall into the trap below. With this in mind it might not always be a negative thing to fall through these platforms.

Environmernt

A series of environmental assets were created to bring the game to life and provide a sense of depth within the environment. The assets were all designed around the theme of death, decay and thereby bones were a central aspect of this. The dark, blurred assets are background and foreground assets, while the lighter ones are means to fill in the middleground.

Gameplay

The game uses a very simple control scheme that translates fluently between publishing platforms.
